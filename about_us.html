ABOUT US

Celebrating a shared passion for exploration, Mara Martin Travel & Co was born from the ever wandering spirits within Nate and Kirst. With a love for travel ingrained in their souls and two cute little cat companions by their side, we set out to craft unique experiences that embody the essence of discovery. Museums, culinary delights, hiking trails, and every hidden gem in between serve as the foundation of our specific itineries with comfort and safety in mind. MMT&Co, we invite you to embark on a journey of connection and discovery, guided by our unwavering commitment to creating unforgettable moments and fostering a deeper appreciation for the world we inhabit.
